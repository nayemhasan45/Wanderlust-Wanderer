    A Simple Perfect Place to Get Lost

    The highest point in Bangladesh is Thaiduangpara, reaching just 1,052 meters (3,451 ft). However, don't let the elevation fool you. The beauty of the Chittagong Hill Tracts lies in its remoteness and unspoiled nature.

    • Stunning scenery: The Chittagong Hill Tracts are home to some of the most beautiful scenery in Bangladesh. The rolling hills are covered in lush green forests, and there are many waterfalls and rivers to explore.

    • Tribal cultures: The Chittagong Hill Tracts are home to a number of different tribal groups, each with its own unique culture and traditions. You can visit tribal villages and learn about their way of life.

    • Adventure activities: There are a number of adventure activities that you can enjoy in the Chittagong Hill Tracts, such as trekking, hiking, and rock climbing.
